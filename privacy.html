SCLAB Privacy and Cookie Policy

Last updated: Jan 1, 2022

The scope of this policy​

SCLAB understands that you are interested in how information about you is collected and used. SCLAB is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who interact with us. SCLAB, Inc. incorporates this SCLAB Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") for our websites, services, and apps linked to this policy ("SCLAB"). This policy also details our privacy practices related to SCLAB marketing, advertising, and company events.

SCLAB is a Digital Transformations visualization management platform. We provide our clients with Digital Transformations visualization authoring tool services and related support, consulting, and other services. The SCLAB platform and related services are collectively referred to as the "Service" in this Privacy Policy. Emails, newsletters, and other marketing and advertising practices are collectively referred to as "communications."

For this Privacy Policy, "Personal Information" means any information relating to an identifiable or identifiable natural person.

We provide Digital Transformation content creation services to our customers. Data from the service is stored in the AWS cloud and MongoDB. When you use our services to process data you collect as to our customers, you control what data we collect, what data is uploaded to the service, and how it is used. The collection, use, transmission, and retention of customer data on our services is governed by our Customer Privacy Policy. As a data processor, we will work with you to control your data.

Information SCLAB Collects​

You may fill out the SCLAB web form and register to use the SCLAB website or service, use service demos, visit the website, use the service, and provide information about you when responding. Advertise, contact SCLAB for support or other offers, or attend events or forums hosted or participated by SCLAB. In this process, we collect personal information about you. For example, we may collect your name, city and company name, address, email, phone number, billing and payment information, title, or title. We may also collect other public and third-party information about you to supplement the information we hold, such as information about the cities and companies you work in.

While using our Services, we may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information that we can use to contact you or identify you. For example, we collect information when you create an account, request customer support, or otherwise communicate with us. The types of information we may collect include basic user information (such as name, email address, social media avatar, phone number, and photo), company information, and other information you choose to provide.

SCLAB website and use of cookies​

We use information collection tools such as cookies and web beacons to collect information when browsing our websites and services. We receive and store information about how you use our websites and services through these tools. For example, we receive information that your browser or device sends to our servers whenever you visit the SCLAB website. Your browser or device is the Internet Protocol (IP) address used to connect your computer to the Internet, the type of computer and browser, version, language and time zone settings, browser plug-in type, operating system, and type of device used. When you visit the SCLAB website, your browser will determine what you have done on our website, the pages you have navigated to our website, and, where applicable, the search terms you enter into the search engine that leads to our website. Our servers collect similar information when you log in to our website or service.

We use first-party and third-party cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies depending on the information and options you provide when these technologies are used. Information received through cookies and similar technologies may be relevant to you, depending on the website or service you use. Third parties may also collect information about your online activities using cookies or other technologies when you use our website or services.

We advertise online, including displaying SCLAB advertisements over the Internet on our websites and apps. When we advertise online to you, we may collect information about the advertisements displayed to you, the advertisements you click on, and the web pages on which the advertisements were displayed.

For details on the types of cookies we use and how to manage cookies, see "Your rights to your personal information" below.

SCLAB websites and services may contain buttons, tools, or links to other companies' services. We may collect information about the use of these features. We know the security of your personal information is essential. We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security controls to protect your personal information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, despite our efforts, there are no complete security controls, and we cannot guarantee or guarantee the security of your personal information.

Your personal information and data files are stored on the cloud servers we use and on the servers of our affiliates and companies that we employ to provide services to us. We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to comply with our statutory obligations and statutory rights, including fraud prevention or contract enforcement, and to provide you with the websites and services you use for the following internal business reasons: Maintenance of our archive and backup systems for some time after the end of your use of our website and services.

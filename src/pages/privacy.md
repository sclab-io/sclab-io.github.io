# SCLAB Privacy and Cookie Policy

_Last updated: Jan 1, 2022_

## The scope of this policy

SCLAB understands that you are interested in how information about you is collected and used. SCLAB is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who interact with us. SCLAB, Inc. incorporates this SCLAB Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") for our websites, services, and apps linked to this policy ("SCLAB"). This policy also details our privacy practices related to SCLAB marketing, advertising, and company events.

SCLAB is a Digital Transformations visualization management platform. We provide our clients with Digital Transformations visualization authoring tool services and related support, consulting, and other services. The SCLAB platform and related services are collectively referred to as the "Service" in this Privacy Policy. Emails, newsletters, and other marketing and advertising practices are collectively referred to as "communications."

For this Privacy Policy, "Personal Information" means any information relating to an identifiable or identifiable natural person.

We provide Digital Transformation content creation services to our customers. Data from the service is stored in the AWS cloud and MongoDB. When you use our services to process data you collect as to our customers, you control what data we collect, what data is uploaded to the service, and how it is used. The collection, use, transmission, and retention of customer data on our services is governed by our Customer Privacy Policy. As a data processor, we will work with you to control your data.
